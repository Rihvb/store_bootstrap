Kyoto - The Heart of Japanese Tradition

Step into a serene world of ancient temples, tea houses, and cherry blossoms.

Overview

Kyoto, Japan's former imperial capital, is a city that whispers stories of an era long past. Nestled among lush mountains, Kyoto is a harmonious blend of Zen gardens, timeless temples, and seasonal beauty. Every corner reveals a new discovery, from the Golden Pavilion’s shimmering reflection to the tranquil pathways of Arashiyama’s bamboo groves. Kyoto is a place where you can experience the essence of Japan’s traditions and spiritual heritage.

Highlights

Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

Admire the beauty of this golden temple set beside a serene pond

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Wander through thousands of vibrant red torii gates leading up the mountain

Gion District

Discover the enchanting world of geishas, tea houses, and traditional streets

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Stroll through towering bamboo stalks in this magical forest

Embark on a journey through Japan’s rich culture and history in Kyoto!
